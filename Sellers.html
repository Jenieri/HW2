Rules and regulations for sellers

Go home.

Shoes must be in Deadstock (brand new) condition, with box and original laces included.

We accept all sizes, but are mostly looking for US men's sizes between 9-15

To sell sneakers you should email Jenieri at jeniericyrus@sneakers.com with a picture of the shoe from the side, from above, and a picture of the sole.

Once your submission has been reviewed, Jenieri will get back to you ASAP with a quote. Offers are non-negotiable.

Jenieri will grant 115% of the quoted offer to be used as store credit if you would like to purchase a sneaker that is in stock.

All shoes go through our in-house authentication system. Cash will not be released until the sneakers are verified to be authentic. Any sneakers that fail authentication will be sent back to seller.

Payment will be facilitated via Venmo, Cash App, or any other payment portal of your choice.

If you have any other questions about selling sneakers, reach out to Jenieri via email at jeniericyrus@sneakers.com or give him a call at 999-999-9999
